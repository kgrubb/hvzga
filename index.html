HvZ GEORGIA SPRING INVITATIONAL

HvZ Georgia's Humans vs. Zombies team is hosting
a spring invitational on Friday March 27, 2019 to Saturday, March 28, 2020
at Nations Church in Athens, Georgia.

Check-in starts at 5:00 p.m. and Mission briefing begins at 6:00 p.m. on Friday.
The Game will end around 2:00 p.m. on Saturday.
