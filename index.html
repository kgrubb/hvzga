HvZ Georgia Spring Invitational

HvZ Georgia's Humans vs. Zombies team is hosting
a spring invitational on Friday April 13, 2018 to Saturday, April 14, 2018
at Church of the Nations, in Athens, Georgia.

Registration starts at 5:00 p.m. and Mission briefing begins at 6:00 p.m. on Friday.
The Game will end around 2:00 p.m. or earlier on Saturday.
